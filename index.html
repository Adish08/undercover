How to Play

Civilians receive the same word. Undercovers receive a similar word. Mr. White gets no word.

Players take turns giving a one-word or short sentence clue about their word. Don't make it too obvious!

After clues, discuss and vote for who you think is Undercover or Mr. White.

Civilians win if they vote out all non-civilians. Undercovers win if they survive. Mr. White wins if they guess the civilian word after being voted out!
